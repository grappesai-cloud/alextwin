The Artist

Alex Twin.

DJ · Producer

Alex Twin continues to ascend in the electronic music scene with his impressive string of releases and captivating DJ sets that have left audiences entranced. His talent and dedication haven't gone unnoticed, as he currently spearheads the music curation for some of Dubai's most iconic venues, such as Ushuaia Dubai and Be Beach Dubai. In these roles, he meticulously assembles great teams to deliver unforgettable musical experiences tailored to each venue's unique vibe.

Known for his dynamic mixing of Electronic, Afro House, Melodic House, and Tech House, Alex brings an unparalleled energy to locations around the globe. Whether performing in Dubai, Ibiza, Mykonos, Monaco, London, or St Tropez, each set is a testament to his adaptability and deep understanding of the crowd.

On the production front, Alex Twin's reputation continues to flourish. His latest releases on Moblack, Madorasindahouse, and Radiate, has gained a significant attention from the audience achieving over 10 million streams and 80 millions views on media channels, sending ripples through the electronic music industry and proving that Alex Twin continues to make all the right moves in his burgeoning DJ and production career.

His work has earned the admiration and support of industry luminaries, and he has shared the DJ booth with heavyweights including &ME, Black Coffee, Arodes, Andrea Oliva, Francis Mercier, Bedouin, Jamie Jones, Ahmed Spins, Damian Lazarus, Pawsa, Adriatique, Michael Bibi, Dennis Cruz, DJ Tennis, and Carlita, cementing his place in the electronic music hall of fame.

With a career dedicated to crafting unique sonic journeys, Alex Twin consistently proves that his passion and artistry has no limits. His journey is one of continuous evolution, consistently captivating audiences with his innovative soundscapes and producing magic wherever he performs.
