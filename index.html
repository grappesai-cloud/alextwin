The Artist

Alex Twin.

DJ · Producer · Music Director at Addmind Group

"Hello, I'm Alex Tudorache — also known as Alex Twin. I've been a DJ and music producer for the past seventeen years, and I'm also involved in music curation and management. I'm passionate about bringing creativity and business together in the music industry."

Music has always been the driving force in his life — guiding him through every phase and shaping who he is today. For the last eleven years that story has been written in Dubai, one of the world's most vibrant cities for nightlife, where he's led venues and groups as both a music director and a DJ. Today he's honoured to work with Addmind Group, a leader of the city's nightlife scene.

As Music Director, he oversees the musical direction of venues and events, making sure they align with each brand's identity — and assembles the teams of DJs who deliver it night after night at Be Beach, Ushuaïa, Clap and several other locations across the region.

Be Beach remains his favourite room in Dubai — a place that transforms seamlessly through the day and turns, as night falls, into a pulsating hub where some of the world's best DJs perform weekly. Recent nights there have put him alongside Andrea Oliva, Shimza, Valeron, Birds of Mind and Pawsa.

His biggest release to date, "Bandido", arrived in summer 2024 and quietly ran away from him — the kind of track that writes its own year. In 2025 he's meticulously crafting his debut album, "Here For Love", set to land by year's end alongside a circle of artists and friends close to his heart — with a handful of new tracks ready to see the light before it.
